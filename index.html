What is Filthy's Nightmare?

Filthy's Nightmare is a survival horror game that is set in 2016 and there will be loads of jumpscares and you will have loads of fun playing the game so stay up to date with development by joining the Discord.



About the Developer

Ethan Wildish/Mr. Robot


Ethan Wildish, or, as most people know him as, Mr. Robot, is a 15 year old aspiring game developer.
Filthy's Nightmare is his first major game project that he hopes to release to the public in early 2019.
In addition to this, Filthy's Nightmare will be made with the well-known and trusted Unity Engine 4.
If you are in need of easily contacting him, you can do so via Twitter, or Discord: Mr. Robot#2221.
